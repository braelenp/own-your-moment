# Own-Your-Moment (OYM)
The First Real Basketball Training dApp on Solana

**Overview**
Own Your Moment is a mobile-first basketball training platform built on Solana. Powered by Swain Basketball (@swain_basketball) and his AAU program, OYM turns training into a sovereign, verifiable, and rewarding experience.
Athletes complete Swain-designed drills, track progress on-chain, mint achievement badges as NFTs, earn $SKR rewards, and share verified stats with college recruiters. The standout feature is King AI — an autonomous assistant that analyzes on-chain stats and curates personalized training plans in real time.

**Key Features**
- Daily Swain drills with interactive tracking
- On-chain progress logging and streak rewards
- Mint NIL achievement badges as NFTs (Metaplex)
- $SKR staking and performance rewards
- Recruiter dashboard with shareable verified stats
- AAU team mode for group progress and Swain live sessions

**King AI Assistant**
- Floating widget on every tab for instant help
- Dedicated analytics tab with recommendations and general questions
- Autonomously suggests drills based on your stats ("Based on your 3PT accuracy, try this footwork drill")

**Tech Stack**
- **Frontend:** React Native + Capacitor (native Android APK for Seeker/Saga phones)
- **On-chain:** Anchor programs for badges, rewards, and NIL contracts
- **NFTs:** Metaplex for achievement badges
- **Wallet:** Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter (native on Seeker)
- **AI Layer:** Verifiable rule-based King AI with on-chain logging

**Roadmap (March Madness Launch)**
**Week 1–2:** Core drills + NFT badges + King AI MVP
**Week 3:** $SKR rewards + recruiter dashboard
**Week 4:** AAU integration + public APK launch
